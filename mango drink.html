Easy Mango Banana Smoothie

This mango banana smoothie is great for kids. As a babysitter, I always look for quick, easy, healthy, and delicious recipes.

Ingredients

  • 2 mangos - peeled, seeded, and sliced
  • 2 bananas
  • 2 cups vanilla yogurt
  • 2 cups milk

Directions

Step 1

Combine mangos, banana, vanilla yogurt, and milk in a blender; blend until smooth.
