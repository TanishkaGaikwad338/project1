Vegan Matcha Latte with Coconut Cream

Refreshing and healthy, this creamy vegan matcha latte with fluffy, whipped coconut cream will keep you energized for a long day!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon sweetened coconut cream, chilled
  • ¼ cup warm water
  • 1 ½ teaspoons green tea powder (matcha)
  • 1 teaspoon agave syrup, or more to taste
  • ¾ cup vanilla-flavored soy milk (such as Soy Dream®), chilled

Directions

Step 1

Whip coconut cream in a chilled bowl with an electric mixer until fluffy and doubled in volume, 30 seconds to 1 minute.

Step 2

Combine warm water and green tea powder in a cup; whisk until dissolved. Stir in agave syrup. Stir in soy milk. Spoon whipped coconut cream on top.
